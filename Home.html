About Us

We are a dedicated team of people who love all things rabbits and rabbit care. We each have had rabbits and want to provide a resource for future and current rabbit owners! Having and caring for a rabbit can be of one of life’s greatest joys, and feeling knowledgeable and prepared is the first step!

Our Purpose

Our purpose with BunnyBase is to allow you to feel prepared in taking care of your rabbit. You have a resource for all things rabbit that can be used as a reference. Additionally, we want to help those who are looking to find their first bun to know which bun will fit in their family the best. The right knowledge and preparation can make all the difference in caring for your new rabbit.
